2012 END OF YEAR CAMPAIGN:

As we come to the end of another year, we are writing to you on behalf of the Galileo Alumni Association to request your help in supporting your Alma Mater. We want to thank those who have contributed in the past and ask if you will help us once again. The holiday season is the perfect time to show how much you care. Our objective is to help support Galileo Academy of Science & Technology students and teachers. With the never ending cuts in budgets, students and teachers are in need of help for equipment and supplies. Your gifts will help ensure that future generations of students benefit from the Galileo experience that has meant so much to all of us. If you would like to contribute, please make an online donation at http://galileoalumni.org/membership/DonateAnyAmount.html or mail to Galileo Alumni Association, 1150 Francisco Street, SF Ca 94109. Thank you for taking the time to read about our annual fund drive. We appreciate your considering our request. Happy Holidays!

2009 Election results:

PRESIDENT’S LETTER November, 23 2009 The G.A.A. is happy to announce the election results of November 19, 2009. Nine G.A.A. members were elected to serve a two year term as directors of the board, commencing January 1, 2010. Those elected were: Augie Venezia (’53) Charlene Fachner Mori (66) Sisvan Der Harootunian (’51) Jesse Figueroa (’60) Monica Kirkland (’53) Lance Hughston (’66) Mario Lechuga (’51) Barbara LaRocca (’69) Lenore LaRocca (’64) Augie, Sisvan and Monica are incumbents, Char, Jesse and Lance are directors previously appointed by the president to fill vacancies and Mario, Barbara and Lenore are newly elected members. Toni Alessandra (’64) was appointed to fill a vacancy with one year remaining on that term. Augie Venezia was retained as association vice president while Char was appointed as Public Relations Committee Chairperson and Toni was appointed as Membership Committee Chairperson. We still have one board position open at this point and all are welcome to volunteer to serve in this two year term. The Association feels that we now have a great blend of experience and new enthusiasm to make 2010 a year of forward thinking and accomplishments. We urge all alumni to be a part of our growth process as we continue our mission to assist and contribute to our Alma Mater.

Galileo Alumni Association website migrating to Galileo Academy website

Posted: October 4, 2008 The Galileo Alumni Association is migrating its website to a new location. Like the PTSA and other organizations closely associated with the school, the new GAA web home will be a branch of the Galileo Academy of Science and Technology website. While the migration  progresses, you may continue viewing GAA web pages at their original location.

You can help! Contact the GAA webmaster to help. You’ll get valuable experience working with WordPress, and will know you’re helping GAA serve it’s mission.
