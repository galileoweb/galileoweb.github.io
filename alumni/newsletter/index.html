The Galileo Observer

The Official Newsletter of the Galileo Alumni Association

The Galileo Observer, the official newsletter of the Galileo Alumni Association, is available to anyone on-line and by email. Registered members of the Association may request delivery by the U.S. Postal Service. For more information see the Membership page.

Want to get the newsletter by email, stop your subscription, or update your email address? Send a message through our Contact Form.
