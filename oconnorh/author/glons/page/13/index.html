Chemistry List of Reaction Types

November 2, 2011 Copernicus Uncategorized

Reaction Types:

Synthesis:   A + B –> AB

Decomposition:  AB –>  A + B

Single Replacement:  A + BC  –> B + AC

Double Displacement:  AB  +  CD  –> AD  +  CB

Combustion:  Carbon (elemental or in a compound) + O2 –> CO2 + H2O

Acid – Base:  Acid + Base –> Salt + Water

Oxidation-Reduction Reactions:    – metal-nonmetal reaction; combustion, synthesis reactions (because reactants are elements), and decomposition reactions (because products are elements) are all oxidation-reduction reactions.
