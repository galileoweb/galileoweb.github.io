Chemistry Lesson Equilibrium/Reaction Rates

May 5, 2011 Copernicus Uncategorized

Standards for today’s lesson:  8.       Chemical reaction rates depend on factors that influence the frequency of collision of reactant molecules. As a basis for understanding this concept:

a.       Students know the rate of reaction is the decrease in concentration of reactants or the increase in concentration of products with time.

b.       Students know how reaction rates depend on such factors as concentration, temperature, and pressure.

c.        Students know the role a catalyst plays in increasing the reaction rate.

Go to http://www.chem4kids.com/files/react_rates.html

  2. Copy the graph that relates rates and reaction and concentration, pressure, and temperature.
  3. Why does an increase in concentration cause an increase in rate of reaction?
  4. Why does an increase in temperature cause an increase in rate of reaction?
  5. Why does an increase in pressure cause an increase in rate of reaction?
