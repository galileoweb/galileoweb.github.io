The act of attending.
The persons or number of persons that are present.
The frequency with which a person is present.

The act of being present (at a class or event etc.) [ant: nonattendance]

The frequency with which a person is present; “a student’s attendance is an important factor in her grade”

Example: His school attendance (= the number of times he attends) is poor; Attendance (= the number of people attending) the class.
