Excuse Notes

July 6, 2015 Copernicus Info

The student note must contain the following information:

  • Student’s First & Last Name
  • Date of Absence & Classes missed
  • Reason for absence (only legally excusable reasons will be accepted)
  • Parent Signature
  • Student Grade
  • Parent Contact Phone Number

Written notes must be in INK. Notes written in pencil cannot be accepted.

Galileo was born in Pisa, Italy, and studied at the University of Pisa (1581-1585). Galileo also found that the speed at which bodies fall does not depend on their weight. He documented these discoveries in his book called, "De Motu" (meaning "On Motion").
