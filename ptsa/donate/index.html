The PTSA supports many programs to benefit the students at Galileo. The PTSA depends on you to help support these programs. There are many ways which you can help, please consider contributing funds, volunteering or participate in fundraising.

Education Fund

The Education Fund is the primary method which the PTSA uses to generate the needed funds to support the many programs at Galileo. Every year during the Fall the PTSA has an Education Fund Drive to encourage the Galileo community to donate to the Education fund. Donations are accepted for the Education Fund at any time during the year. Please support the programs at Galileo by donating to the Education Fund. For details on donating to the Education Fund, please download this form.

Education Fund Form

Corporate Matching Contribution
Your Education Fund contribution can go twice as far!  Your contribution may be matched by your employer or former employer.  Check the Matching Donor List and see if your company’s name is there. Even if it’s not, contact your Human Resources administrator for details on how your company can join you in supporting Galileo Academy.

Please use the Tax ID # and mail address in the Education Fund Form and be sure that the matching fund donation is made payable to Galileo Academy PTSA.   Please submit an Education Fund Form to notify us of your donation.

Lucky S.H.A.R.E.S Card

How would you like to earn money for Galileo Academy while shopping for groceries?

    In your message indicate you want a Lucky SHARES card and include your name and child’s name.

You can also go directly to the eScrip Online Mall for Galileo by clicking on the logo below.

 

 

 

Safeway

Safeway regularly supports Galileo by donating food and drinks for our events.

Mollie Stone
You can earn up to 5% every time your shop at Mollie Stone’s. Visit Mollie Stone’s and request a Community Card. Go to the eScrip site to register your community card. Shop at Mollie Stone’s and show your Community Card when you check out. Mollie Stone’s will automatically transfer up to 5% based on your total purchases to Galileo.

 

One Cause

One Cause donate funds everytime you make an purchase from an on-line Merchant via the One Cause website.  Join One Cause and designate Galileo by clicking on the logo below.

 

Target REDCard 

All you have to do is use your Target® Visa® or Target® Debit REDcard. Galileo will receive a percentage of the amount of each purchase in the form of a cash donation from Target through the Take Charge of Education® program. It’s easy. You must designate Galileo Academy when signing up under the Take Charge of Education program. So if you’re going to Target anyway, please consider using your Target® Visa® or Target® Debit REDcard.

Sports Basement
Need sports equipment or supplies? Shop at Sport Basement and mention Galileo at the checkout and you receive 10% off your purchases and the school receives 5%.

Cole Hardware

Galileo will get a gift card equal to 10% of all purchases made throughout the year. Volunteer your Skills and time

Your support is important and will make a difference in our community! Bilingual parents especially needed. Email us if you want to volunteer or have any questions. Please include in the email where you can volunteer.

Here are some areas which we need help:

  • Fundraising: Help with Education Fund Drive & other fundraising activities.
  • Membership: Help with paperwork, conduct membership drive.
  • Mailing/Clerical: Help PTSA and the school with duplication, fold & mail newsletters, etc.
  • Staff Appreciation: Help plan and conduct annual appreciation event for Galileo’s staff in May.
  • Serve on PTSA Board or Committees
  • Technology: Help with IT projects & PTSA website
  • Other short term special projects
