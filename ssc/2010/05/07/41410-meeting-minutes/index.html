Proposition A Bond School Greening

Middle and High School Program Overview

Program Summary:

The Green Schoolyard Grant Program is meant to provide Middle and High Schools that are listed in the 2003 Proposition A Bond with the opportunity to transform a portion of their school’s outdoor area into a green schoolyard.

Eligible schools have the opportunity to apply for a grant of up to $100,000 to install green schoolyard elements on school grounds. The school community will be required to work with a landscape architect to design their green schoolyard project.  Any green schoolyard elements installed on school grounds MUST be ADA accessible.  Funds: up to $100,000

Program Timeline

May 14 2010

Deadline to submit completed application and supplemental documents.

June 2 2010

Selection committee identifies/notifies successful applicants.

August 16, 2010 thru February 15, 2011

Design Process with Landscape Architect

  • During the Fall/Winter of 2010 -11 the school community will work closely with a Landscape Architect (selected by SFUSD) to develop a design for the school’s green schoolyard project.
  • The design process will consist of a site visit with the Landscape Architect and a minimum of three meetings between the school’s greening committee, the landscape architect, and SFUSD Bond staff where the designs will be developed and finalized.
  • The landscape architect working with your school must submit the final drawings and any other relevant documents for your green schoolyard to SFUSD’s Bond Program by February 15, 2011.

March, 2011 through early May, 2011

SFUSD conducts the “bidding” process to select/hire a general/landscape contractor who will implement the design developed by the school community and the landscape architect.

June 2011 through August 2011

Selected landscape/general contractor conducts construction on school grounds.

Application Process

Your school community will need to have an understanding of:

  • Any existing school site greening features
  • Your project’s vision
  • What kinds of green schoolyard infrastructure you want
  • Location(s) of functioning water sources near proposed greening areas if water access is necessary
  • What kind of programming you propose
  • How you plan to maintain your project
  • Any PE programming at your school and how it would be affected

Please read through the application and answer questions thoroughly!

Required Items

  • Completed Application (including all required signatures)
  • Existing Conditions Site Plan
  • Proposed Green Schoolyard Site Plan

(Proposed locations of green schoolyard elements are subject to change due to design and ADA constraints.)

  • Signed Maintenance Agreement(s)
