4/15/09 Meeting Minutes

April 20, 2009 Karin Lau Meeting Minutes

San Francisco Unified School District
Galileo Academy of Science and Technology
1150 Francisco Street
San Francisco, CA  94109

Room 210 at 3:20 P.M.
Location and Time of Meeting

School Site Council Meeting-April 15, 2009
X  Agenda Attached
X  Attendance/sign-in attached
Present:  Vicki Pesek, Ben Chun, Doug Page, Jonathan Ring, Bettie Grinnell, Leland Wong, Linda Liu, Dominic Tsang, Jessica Wong, Samantha Lee
Absent (Excused):  Stan DeBella, Mele Lau-Smith, Audrey Ricci, Kathleen Golata
X  Minutes of 2 previous meetings approved

Decision Made/Issues Reviewed

I.  Call to Order
–B. Grinnell, SSC chair, called the meeting to order at 3:30 pm.

II.  Approval of 3/11/2009 and 3/25/2009 Meeting Minutes
— D. Tsang corrected the 3/11 minutes-section VI. New Business, item 2- Extended Calendar Request Deadline to clarify his comment:  His suggestion was that the nomination process should be similar to the Jefferson Awards nomination process, not that any Jefferson Award recipients should get cash rewards.
— J. Wong moved to approve the minutes as amended.  D. Page seconded the motion, and all voted in favor.

III.  Principal’s Report
— The Board of Education voted in closed session to oppose Fiona Ma’s proposal, meaning that there will be no PE credits for JROTC.
–Galileo has 70 appeals with 350 on the waiting list, and 2135 students enrolled.  We’re still trying to get a few more to allow for attrition.

IV.  Old Business
1. 2009-10 Preliminary Budget Update
–V. Pesek will be meeting with Nancy Waymack to try for more SCE funding to help preserve teaching positions.

V.  New Business
1.  Prop H PE budget Plans
–Ethan Winterling presented a preliminary budget for Proposition H funds for the PE department.  He also explained the background of Proposition H (which designates funds for SLAM – Sports, Libraries, Arts, and Music).  He said that we’re generally in good shape with equipment for athletics and sports.  The main focus right now is on the fitness lab and expanding it with new machines.  The exact spending within the budget is done within the department, using the stations and activities to structure the decisions.  S. Lee asked about Dragon Boat funding and was encouraged to pursue Proposition H.

2. Interview Questions
–April 25 interviews for AP curriculum require us to develop a set of questions to ask the candidates.  B. Grinnell passed out questions that were used in 2007 to interview for AP pupil services.  D. Page suggested that we focus on the most important aspects of the job in formulating the questions.  SSC members worked together to refine a list of 7 questions.  B. Chun read the questions, D. Page moved to accept the questions as read, and J. Wong seconded the motion.  All voted in favor.

VI.  Open Forum
— L. Liu raised the proposed 2010 calendar change and the students present all agreed that they are in favor of the change.

D. Tsang moved to adjourn.  S. Lee seconded the motion and the meeting was adjourned at 4:40 pm.

Minutes taken and prepared by Ben Chun
