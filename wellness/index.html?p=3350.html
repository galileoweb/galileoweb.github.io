Teen Clinics

San Francisco Teen Clinics

The Galileo Wellness Program can help you make a confidential clinic appointment or you can call and make one on your own.

If you want help making an appointment come to Wellness and ask Naomi (Rm. 151), Michelle (151B) or Nurse Kitchell (Rm. 159)

  • Larkin Street Clinic – (415) 673-0911 (x259)
    • No insurance needed or Medi-Cal
  • New Generation Health Center – (415) 502-8336
    • No insurance needed
  • Planned Parenthood Valencia St. Clinic – (415) 821-1282
    • No insurance needed or Healthy SF
  • Women’s Community Clinic ~ (415) 379-7800
    • No insurance needed
  • Huckleberry Youth Health Center’s Cole St. Clinic – (415) 751-8181
    • No insurance needed
  • Dimensions Clinic @ Castro Mission Heath Center for LGBTQ Youth – (415) 487-7554
    • No insurance needed
  • 3rd St. Youth Center and Clinic – (415) 822-1707
    • No insurance needed
  • Kaiser Teen Clinic – (415) 833-3443 or 1-415-833-8336 (Main Number 1-415-833-2200)
    • You need Kaiser insurance but it is separate from your family’s insurance

Teens in California have many rights when it comes to health! Know Your Rights – click here for more information.

What services can you get a teen clinic?

Clinics can offer teens confidential (private) care & support including:

  • Pregnancy testing and counseling
  • Emergency Contraception – “The Morning After Pill” or Plan B
  • Birth control 
  • Free condoms
  • STD testing and treatment
  • HIV testing and counseling
  • Annual pelvic exams and pap smears for women
  • Testicular exams for men
  • Health information
Want to know more about your rights to medical care as someone under 18? Click here: CA Minor Consent Laws 

Not sure how to get to the clinic on MUNI? Check out:Trip Planner or Google Maps

To get helping making an appointment or to ask question about teen clinics, stop by the Galileo Wellness Center in Room 151 Monday-Friday during lunch or after school (drop-in hours). All Wellness services are free & confidential. We are here to support you!
