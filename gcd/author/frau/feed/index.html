As Samantha said in the meeting today that we have to edit our profile and use our name.

If you are having trouble follow this guide:
First login to your yahoo! group account.
Locate and click on “Edit Membership” near the top of the page.
Locate and click “(Add new profile)” under “Yahoo! Profile”
Then, you will have to enter your password.
Go to the “Aliases” section and click “Add an alias”
Type in your name, for example, bruce_lee or bruce_lee99, and hit save.
Click close at the end of the page and your edit membership page will reappear.
Lastly, click “Save Changes” and you are done!

Hope this post help for those that was in trouble.
