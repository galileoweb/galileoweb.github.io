Electronics Recycling, 9/19

August 31, 2015 machtayh Uncategorized
Electronics Recycling Drive
Saturday, September 19, 2015
9am – 1pm
Galileo Parking Lot @ Polk & Bay St
Recycle your old electronics and drop them off at Galileo! We accept computers, monitors, keyboards, phones, printers, cartridges, and more! If you have a bunch of old electronics at your work place, bring them to Galileo! You can drop off anytime prior and during the event!
Students can bring in old electronics anytime during the week of September 14th. Bring your electronics to the ASB Store, room 150.
FREE PICKUP FOR LARGE QUANTITIES
If you have more than 20 TV’s PCs and/or Monitors, you can schedule a free pickup. In order for the school to receive credit, you must call before Saturday to schedule a pick up and mention Galileo Academy. The pick-up can occur after Saturday, but it must be scheduled before our event date on Saturday. You can contact AllGreenRecycling at 800-780-047 or visit AllGreenRecycling.com for more information.

About Galileo Academy

The Galileo Academy of Science and Technology provides students with career pathways in biotechnology, environmental science, health, hospitality and tourism, computer science, and creative media technology.

A San Francisco public high school, Galileo Academy is part of the San Francisco Unified School District - SFUSD.
