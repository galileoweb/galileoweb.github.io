Student Organizations (Clubs)

Galileo has over 40 Clubs and Sport Teams* which provide you various way to GET INVOLVED! Join a club or team now!

Student Organization: Clubs, Sport Teams*, and other Student Supportive Programs

Student Organizations are manage by Executive Office of Student Organization Management of Associated Student Body.

Instructions – School Year 2015-2016

Organizations, in order to be recognized, must fill in the Form C-1. [Click here to download]

To participate in school festivals, organization must be recognized and turn in Form C-2 to reserve for the participation. This form is due the Tuesday before the event takes place. [Click here to download]

An organization must be sponsored by at least one certified faculty or staff.
