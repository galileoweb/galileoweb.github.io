Shadowing

July 6, 2015 Copernicus Uncategorized

The Shadowing Program at Galileo Academy of Science & Technology for incoming 8th graders will begin on Tuesday, October 14, 2014, and will continue on most Tuesdays through Dec. 9, 2014 (excluding school holidays). Please fill out this form to schedule a shadow date: tinyurl.com/galileo2019 Galileo Shadow Form Once again, we highly suggest you to attend a school tour before you schedule a shadow date. School Tours

July 6, 2015 Copernicus Uncategorized

Galileo school tours will take place every Friday from October 3, 2014 to January 16, 2015. October tour dates are October 3rd, 10th, 17th, 24th and 31st. November tour dates are November 7th, 14th, and 21st. December tour dates are December 5th and 12th. January tour dates are January 9th and 16th. Our tours begin at 9:00 am and end by 10:00 am. No appointments are required — just come to the Main Office at 8:45 am. Galileo is located at 1150 Francisco Street.

Parents/Students should attend a School Tour before they shadow.

The SFUSD Enrollment Fair will be on Saturday, October 25, 2014, from 9:30 am to 2:30pm pm at John O’Connell High School, located at 2355 Folsom Street, San Francisco.
