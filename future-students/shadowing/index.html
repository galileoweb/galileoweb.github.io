SHADOWING AT GALILEO: What You Need to Know

Parents/Students should attend a School Tour before they shadow.

  1. Shadowing is permitted on the following TUESDAYS ONLY (NO EXCEPTIONS) in 2014: October 14, 21, and 28;  November 4, 11, and 18, December 2, 9
    • Option A:  Shadowing begins at 8:00am and ends at 12:15pm.
    • Option B:  Shadowing begins at 8:00am and ends at 3:10pm.
  2. Shadowing is processed through the Galileo Connection Office ONLY (Room 209), please fill out this form if interested in participating in Galileo Shadow program: tinyurl.com/galileo2019
  3. Visiting Non-Galileo students should try to find a Galileo student to shadow (we will  find someone for you if you do not know any Galileo students).
  4. Galileo students will pickup a Shadowing Permission Form from Mr. Bai Zhen in Room 209.
  5. The permission form is then filled out with the name of the Visiting student who wishes to shadow with the Galileo student.
  6. The Galileo student then takes the permission form to ALL of his/her teachers for them to sign off and grand their permission for a Visiting student to be permitted in their classes for a day.
  7. After the form has been signed by ALL the teachers, it is returned to Mr. Bai Zhen in Room 209, along with a permission letter from the Visiting student’s parent (with a valid phone number).
  8. The Galileo and Visiting students will report to the Main Office on the day they are scheduled and the visiting student will be given a Visitor Pass.
  9. For more information, please call Mr. Bai Zhen at 415 749-3430 x3231 or e-mail him at: zhenb@galileoweb.org
