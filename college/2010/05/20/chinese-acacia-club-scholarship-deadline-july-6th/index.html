Chinese Acacia Club Scholarship – Deadline July 6th

May 20, 2010 College & Career Fairs

Eligibility:

  • Graduating high school senior with acceptance to a post secondary institution that requires for admission graduation from high school
  • Has a GPA of 3.8 or higher
  • Current transcript or report card from last semester
  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher or counselor

Application:

  1. Pick up the application from Ms. Boran in the counseling office
  2. Mail to:
Mr. Fred Kao
Chinese Acacia Club Scholarship Fund
P.O. Box 19165
Oakland, CA 94619
Telephone/Fax: 510-357-1177

Award: $3,000.00

DUE: APPLICATION MUST BE POST MARKED NO LATER THAN TUESDAY, JULY 6, 2010 TO BE CONSIDERED.
