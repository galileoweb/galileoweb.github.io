Evolution Comic Strip

Make 4-6 panels (squares).  Draw pictures of 2 members of the same species, that have some sort of variation (i.e. polar bear brothers that have different fur color:   a polar bear with white fur and a polar bear with brown spots).

On the panels, show how one of the variations allows one of them to survive better than the other.  Show the progression of how over time the environment selects the favorable trait.
