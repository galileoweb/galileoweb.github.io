Homework due Tue., 9/15/09

September 14, 2009 petersj 12th grade English

Read chapters 15 and 16 of Brave New World.

Write the satire letter, one page.  If you type it, double-space.

  • Write a personal letter to the author from any one of the main characters approving or disapproving of his satire.  Use the voice of the character.  If you are Lenina, you might be on soma and complacent.  If you are Bernard from the beginning of the book, you will probably praise Huxley for his efforts to make this world look ridiculous.
  • You must use at least four of the following terms in your letter: soma, hypnopaedic or hypnopaedia, pneumatic, conditioning, Utopia or utopian, Ford, Bokanovsky or bokanovskification, stability, community, identity, individual.

Homework due Mon., 9/14/09

September 11, 2009 petersj 9th grade English

Read “The Most Dangerous Game” on p. 19 of your workbook.

Homework due Mon., 9/14/09

September 11, 2009 petersj 12th grade English

Read chp. 13 and 14 of Brave New World.

John clashes with the people of the new world.  Describe three specific incidents from chapters eleven and twelve where John clashes with someone in the new world, explain the reason for the clash, and explain the reasoning of the person(s) with whom John clashes.

Homework due Fri., 9/11/09

September 11, 2009 petersj 12th grade English

On the back of the timeline, write your name in the middle and surround it with adjectives that describe you, both strengths and weaknesses.  Then, write a paragraph based on your description of you with what you would like to reveal about yourself.

In another paragraph,  choose at least three of your weaknesses and explain how they can be turned into strengths.
