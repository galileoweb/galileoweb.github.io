Homework due Fri., 3/12/10

March 10, 2010 petersj 12th grade English

Hamlet assignment: In I: iii, 60-87 (small book), or 55-87 (big book) of Hamlet Polonius says to his son Laertes, “To thine own self be true.”  Answer the following questions in your own words and interpretations by writing two thoughtfully written paragraphs.

  • Understanding the context (what’s going on) of this scene, what do you think Polonius means when he says “To thine own self be true?”
  • What does “To thine own self be true” mean to you?
  • Do you think that this quote from Hamlet may be a foreshadowing of some sort?
  • Do you think that this quote may be Shakespeare’s way of teaching us something?  If so, what?
