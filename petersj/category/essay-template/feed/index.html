Remember the following while using this diagram:

  • Not all essays are five paragraphs. Some are three, some are twenty.
  • Keep your concrete details, which are usually quotes from text, short and sweet. You are the writer here, not the author of the book you are using for evidence.
  • Use the rules of formal writing; i.e., no slang or contractions unless it is in a quote, avoid using “I think,” act as if what you are writing is right and do not waver in your opinion, have a creative title.
