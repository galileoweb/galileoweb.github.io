On the left:

“Quote from the text” ( author’s last name page number).

On the right:

Text to self, world, or text connection

Context of the quote.

Explanation of the connection.

Question about the text.

Example:

On the left: “My father has asked me to be the fourth corner at the Joy Luck Club” (Tan 5).

On the right: Text to self

My father asked me to be involved in understanding my own mother’s culture as well.  This was difficult for me.

What does Jing-Mei’s father want for her to learn?
