Read “The Moon Lady” from The Joy Luck Club. Answer the following in one paragraph:

What role does water play in this chapter? What does it symbolize?

On the same piece of paper: Write a comprehensive sentence for each vocabulary word from class on Tuesday. Underline the vocabulary word in the sentence.

November 26, 2008 petersj 10th grade English

Read the chapter entitled “The Red Candle” in The Joy Luck Club.

Respond to the following in at least two paragraphs:

Explain the role of women in this chapter.  What power do they have?  How do they manifest this power?

  • Chinese immigration throughout the world from 1929  to 1960.
