Write a Letter to Yourself

 

Write a letter to your future self.  It is confidential (I promise I will not be reading it). This letter will be mailed to you in a year. Be thoughtful and specific.

 

Address the following topics in your letter in complete sentences:

 

  1. 1.     How do you hope you will be different from the person you are today? Describe how you imagine you will have changed.
  2. 3.     What are your current worries and concerns?
  3. 4.     What advice would you give to your older and wiser future self?
  1. What are the different dreams and goals you would want realized by then?
    1. E.g. Career/ Studies? Finances? Family? Friends? Love? Health? Spirituality? Recreation? Personal growth? Contribution?
  1. What do you hope to achieve by the time you read this letter?
  2. What would you like to tell your 21-self? What do you hope to learn by this time? What words of wisdom would you give your 30-self?
  3. What are some memorable and funny things you want to remember for the future?
  4. What are some things you’re grateful for?
  5. Date & address the letter to your future self.
  6. If you want, decorate your letter and its envelope.
