]]> http://galileoweb.org/ungv/2009/04/23/3-imperialism-around-the-world-presentation-due-f-424/feed/ 0 High School Education Extra Credit DUE: F 4/3 http://galileoweb.org/ungv/2009/03/11/high-school-education-extra-credit-due-f-43/ http://galileoweb.org/ungv/2009/03/11/high-school-education-extra-credit-due-f-43/#comments Thu, 12 Mar 2009 00:31:23 +0000 http://old.galileoweb.org/ungv/?p=271 High School Education Extra Credit (50pts) DUE: F 4/3

In light of the slumping economy and budget cuts to San Francisco public schools, I am offering an extra credit assignment since you are the future voters of the U.S.

Choose a position for this essay topic:  Would you send your own children to a United States public school?

Compare and contrast the public high school education system in the U.S. to another country’s (of your choosing).

United States:

Research how much of the U.S. budget is devoted to education.  How much funding actually goes to U.S. public schools a year.  How much do you feel Americans value education for their children and teacher according to your findings.

The Other Country:

How much is education funded in another country?  How important are teachers in that country?  How valued is education? Provide detailed examples to illustrate your point.

Personally

Finally, discuss how the lack of funding for public schools personally affects your life.  What does this mean to your future?  What does it mean for the future of the U.S.? Do you think you have control over your education and how successful you become?  Do you have friends or family who attend private school?  How is their education similar or different?  Provide detailed examples and/or personal accounts to illustrate your point.

As a future voter, how can you change/influence public education?

Formats:

You may present your findings in the following formats (If you choose to do something else, please speak to me about it first).

  1. Storybook (addressing the issues)
  2. Poster/Collage (addressing the issues)
  3. Essay (2-3 pages, typed, double space, MUST BE WRITTEN IN OWN WORDS, and cite sources!)

Assignments that look slapped together at the last minute will not receive credit.  If you choose to do this assignment, show your sincerity (and also creativity).  Please feel free to ask me questions.  And…. pace yourself!
