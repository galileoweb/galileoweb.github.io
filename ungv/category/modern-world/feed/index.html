Modern World Final Debate Topics

  1. 1. India was better off separated by religious affiliation. (i.e. Pakistan, Bangladesh & India)
    1. Consider Gandhi’s vision, conflict within India (beginning with ancient Indian history), British imperialism in India

  1. 2. Was Mao Zedong a dictator or a revolutionary?
    1. Mao’s rise to power
    2. The Great Leap Forward & the Cultural Revolution
  2. 3. Which government is best at bringing order to society—dictatorship or democracy?
    1. Consider absolute rulers and dictators that have risen to power throughout history (E.g. Napoleon, Stalin, Hitler, Mao, Castro, etc.)
    2. Consider democratic governments & their effectiveness
    3. Consider previous v. later form of government
    4. E.g. Imperial Britain in India v. Nazi Party takeover of Germany
  3. 4. Was imperialistic rule was beneficial to its colonies?
    1. Imperialism in Africa, Asia & South America

    C. 13 World War I —Study Guide

    Know the following terms & concepts:

    1.     Militarism

    2.     nationalism

    3.     Kaiser Wilhelm II

    4.     Event that triggered WWI

    5.     Triple Alliance & Triple Entente

    6.     Central Powers & Allies

    7.     Schlieffen Plan

    8.     Unrestricted submarine warfare

    9.     Trench warfare

    10.  U.S. & Zimmermann telegram

    11.  U.S. entry in WWI & effects

    12.  Total war

    13.  Armistice

    14.  Fourteen Points

    15.  Treaty of Versailles & long-term effects

    16.  European economy

    Essay Questions:

    Prepare for both essay questions but only ONE will be on your exam.

    1.     Synthesizing World War I is considered a major turning point in history.  How did the war change the nature of warfare, the map of Europe, and the outlook of modern society?

    2.     Evaluating Decisions What mistakes were made by the leaders who made the Treaty of Versailles?
